Simple Atomic Structure

What is an Atom? ⚛️

An atom is the smallest unit of matter. They have a central core called the nucleus and tiny electrons that orbit it.

Inside the Atom

  • Protons (p⁺): Positive particles in the nucleus. The number of protons determines the element.
  • Neutrons (n⁰): Neutral particles in the nucleus.
  • Electrons (e⁻): Negative particles that orbit the nucleus in layers called shells.

How to Play the Games

  • Easy (Guess the Element): Identify an element from its atomic number or electron arrangement.
  • Medium (Particle Sort): Drag protons, neutrons, and electrons to their correct location in an atom.
  • Hard (Identify the Atom): Identify an element by looking at its full atomic diagram.
