Chemical Kinetics

Reaction Rate

The speed at which a chemical reaction occurs. It's affected by several factors.

Collision Theory

For a reaction to occur, particles must collide with sufficient energy (Activation Energy) and in the correct orientation.

Factors Affecting Rate

  • Temperature: Higher temp = faster particles = more effective collisions.
  • Concentration: More particles = more frequent collisions.
  • Catalyst: Provides an alternative pathway with lower activation energy.
