Simple Chemical Reactions

What is a Chemical Reaction? 🧪

A chemical reaction is a process that transforms one set of chemical substances (reactants) to another (products).

Common Types of Reactions

  • Combination: Two reactants combine to form one product. (A + B ⟶ AB)
  • Decomposition: One compound breaks down. (AB ⟶ A + B)
  • Single Displacement: An element swaps places with another in a compound.
  • Double Displacement: Parts of two compounds swap places to form new compounds.
