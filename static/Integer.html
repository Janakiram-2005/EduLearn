Understanding Integers

What Are Integers?

Integers are all whole numbers, including zero, and their negative counterparts. They do not include fractions or decimals. Examples: ...-3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3...

The Number Line

A number line is the best way to visualize integers. Zero is in the middle. Positive numbers are to the right, and negative numbers are to the left.

Adding & Subtracting Integers

  • Adding a positive: Move to the RIGHT. (e.g., –3 + 5 = 2).
  • Adding a negative: Move to the LEFT. (e.g., 7 + (–9) = -2).
  • Subtracting a negative: Two negatives make a positive! 5 - (-2) becomes 5 + 2 = 7.
