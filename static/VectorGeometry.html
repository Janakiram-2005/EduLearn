Vectors & 3D Geometry

Core Concepts

  • Vectors: Quantities with both magnitude and direction.
  • Scalar (Dot) Product: a · b = |a||b|cos(θ). Result is a scalar.
  • Vector (Cross) Product: a × b. Result is a vector perpendicular to both a and b.
  • Lines in 3D: Defined by a point and a direction vector.
  • Planes in 3D: Defined by a point and a normal vector.
