Sound: The Physics of Waves

Sound is a form of energy that travels through matter as vibrations, creating waves our ears can detect.

Properties of Sound Waves

  • Frequency: Determines the pitch (how high or low a sound is). Measured in Hertz (Hz).
  • Amplitude: Determines the loudness of the sound. Measured in Decibels (dB).

Game Modes

  • Easy: Basic concepts and definitions.
  • Medium: Human hearing range and soundproofing.
  • Hard: Advanced physics principles of sound.
