Biomolecules & Polymers

Biomolecules are the essential organic molecules in living organisms. They are the building blocks of life!

  • Monomers: Small, simple molecules (e.g., glucose, amino acids).
  • Polymers: Large molecules made by linking many monomers (e.g., starch, proteins).

How to Play the Games

  • Easy (Invasion Defense): Direct moving monomers to their correct polymerization site.
  • Medium (Lab Escape): Solve puzzles about monomers and polymers to escape the lab.
  • Hard (Enzyme Strategy): Place enzymes on a grid to efficiently convert substrates into products.
