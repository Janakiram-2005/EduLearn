Chemical Equilibrium

What is Equilibrium? ⚖️

Chemical equilibrium is a state in a reversible reaction where the rate of the forward reaction equals the rate of the reverse reaction. This means the concentrations of reactants and products remain constant over time.

Le Chatelier's Principle

This principle states that if a change (like temperature, pressure, or concentration) is applied to a system at equilibrium, the system will shift to counteract the change and establish a new equilibrium.
