Carbon Compounds

What are Carbon Compounds? 🌿

Organic chemistry is the study of carbon compounds. Carbon is special because it can form strong bonds with other atoms, creating millions of different compounds essential for life.

Key Concepts

  • Hydrocarbons: Compounds made of only carbon and hydrogen. Saturated hydrocarbons (like CH₄) have only single bonds. Unsaturated ones (like C₂H₄) have double or triple bonds.
  • Functional Groups: A specific group of atoms within a molecule that determines its properties, like -OH (alcohols) and -COOH (carboxylic acids).

How to Play the Games

  • Medium (Name the Compound): Identify a compound from its structure.
