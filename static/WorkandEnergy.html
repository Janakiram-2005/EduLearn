Work & Energy: Concepts and Applications

Work and energy are fundamental concepts in physics that describe how forces cause changes in motion and how energy is transferred and transformed.

What is Work?

  • Potential Energy: Stored energy due to position
  • Thermal Energy: Energy from heat
  • Chemical Energy: Energy stored in chemical bonds

Law of Conservation of Energy

Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only converted from one form to another. The total energy in a closed system remains constant.

Power

Power is the rate at which work is done or energy is transferred:

Power = Work / Time
