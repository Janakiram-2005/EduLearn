🚜 Farm Manager Briefing

Your goal is to make the best decisions across a full farming cycle to achieve a high crop yield. Each decision affects your final harvest.

The Farming Cycle

  1. Variety Selection: Choose your seed.
  2. Soil Preparation: Prepare the field for sowing.
  3. Nutrient Management: Add fertilizer or manure.
  4. Water Management: Irrigate your crops.
  5. Pest Control: Defend against harmful insects.
  6. Weed Management: Protect your crop from competing weeds.

Your choices will affect the **Growth Meter**. Good luck!
