Welcome to the bloodstream! You are a White Blood Cell, the last line of defense against invaders. Survive as long as you can in this hostile environment.

How to Play

  • Use the **Left and Right Arrow Keys** to move your cell.

  • The game ends when your health runs out. Go for a high score!

Difficulty Levels

Higher difficulties increase the speed of falling items and the number of pathogens you'll face.
