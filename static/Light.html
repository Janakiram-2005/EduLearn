Light: Properties and Behavior

Light is a form of energy that travels in waves. Understanding its behavior is fundamental to physics.

Key Properties

  • Reflection: Bouncing off surfaces (like a mirror).
  • Refraction: Bending when passing through different materials (like a straw in water).
  • Dispersion: Separating into different colors (like a rainbow).
