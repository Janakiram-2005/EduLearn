Cybersecurity Basics

Internet Terms

  • IP Address: A unique address for your device on the internet.
  • Firewall: A security guard for your network that blocks unauthorized access.
  • Encryption: Scrambling data so only authorized parties can read it.

Common Threats

Be aware of these common cyber threats:

  • Malware/Virus: Harmful software designed to damage your computer.
  • Phishing: Fake emails or messages designed to trick you into giving away personal information.
  • DDoS Attack: Overloading a website with traffic to make it crash.
