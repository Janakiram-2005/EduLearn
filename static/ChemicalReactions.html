Chemical Reactions

What is a Chemical Reaction?

A process that transforms one set of chemical substances to another. Signs include color change, gas formation, or precipitate formation.

Types of Reactions

  • Combination: A + B → AB
  • Decomposition: AB → A + B
  • Displacement: A + BC → AC + B
  • Double Displacement: AB + CD → AD + CB

Balancing Equations

The Law of Conservation of Mass means the number of atoms of each element must be the same on both sides of an equation.
