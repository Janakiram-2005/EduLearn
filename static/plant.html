The Journey of a Plant

1. Plant Classification

Plants are grouped by size and stem type into Herbs, Shrubs, Trees, Climbers, and Creepers.

2. Parts of a Plant

Roots anchor the plant and absorb water. The stem supports the plant and transports water and nutrients.

3. Photosynthesis

4. Transpiration

Transpiration is how plants lose excess water as vapor from their leaves.

5. Reproduction

Pollination, often helped by bees, is key for plants to produce seeds.

6. Seed Germination

This is the process where a seed sprouts and grows into a seedling.

7. Importance of Plants

Plants are vital! They provide us with oxygen, food, wood, and medicines.
