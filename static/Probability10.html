The Science of Chance

What is Probability?

Probability is the measure of how likely an event is to happen. It is always a number between 0 (impossible) and 1 (certain).

The Formula

The basic formula for probability is:

P(event) = (Favorable Outcomes) / (Total Outcomes)

Example: The probability of rolling a 4 on a six-sided die is $1/6$.
