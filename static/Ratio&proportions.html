Understanding Ratios & Proportions

What is a Ratio?

A ratio is a way to compare two quantities. If you have 2 apples and 3 oranges, the ratio of apples to oranges is 2 to 3, written as 2:3 or 2/3.

What is a Proportion?

A proportion is an equation stating that two ratios are equal. For example, the ratio 1/2 is equal to 2/4. So, 1/2 = 2/4 is a proportion.

Solving for a Missing Value

To solve a proportion like 2 : 5 = ? : 20, find the relationship between the known parts.

  1. Compare the second numbers: 5 and 20.
  2. To get from 5 to 20, you multiply by 4.
  3. Do the same to the first number: 2 x 4 = 8.
  4. The complete proportion is 2 : 5 = 8 : 20.
