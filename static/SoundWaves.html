Sound Waves

Sound is a form of energy that travels through matter as waves. These waves are created by vibrating objects and can be heard when they reach our ears.

Properties of Sound Waves

  • Frequency: The number of vibrations per second (determines pitch).
  • Amplitude: The size of the vibrations (determines loudness).
  • Wavelength: The distance between waves.

How Sound Travels

Sound waves require a medium (solid, liquid, or gas) to travel through. They travel fastest through solids, slower through liquids, and slowest through gases.
