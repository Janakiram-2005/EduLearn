What are Polygons?

A polygon is any flat, 2D shape that is fully closed and made up of straight sides. The word "Polygon" comes from Greek, with "Poly" meaning "many" and "gon" meaning "angle".

Naming Polygons

We name polygons based on how many sides they have:

  • Triangle: 3 sides
  • Quadrilateral: 4 sides
  • Pentagon: 5 sides
  • Hexagon: 6 sides
