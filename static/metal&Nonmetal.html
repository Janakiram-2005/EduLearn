Metals & Non-Metals

Properties of Metals 🪙

Metals are usually solid, shiny, and good conductors of heat and electricity. Examples include Iron, Copper, and Silver.

Properties of Non-Metals 🌱

Non-Metals are typically not shiny and are poor conductors. They can be solids, liquids, or gases. Examples include Oxygen, Carbon, and Sulphur.

How to Play the Games

  • Easy (Sorter): Drag elements into the 'Metals' or 'Non-Metals' bins.
  • Medium (Conductivity Test): Drag a material to see if it lights up the bulb.
  • Hard (Reaction Simulator): Mix elements with reactants and predict the outcome.
