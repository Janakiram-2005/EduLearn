Understanding Exponents

What Are Exponents?

Exponents, or "powers," are a shortcut for repeated multiplication. Instead of writing 5 × 5 × 5, you can write 53.

  • The Base: The number being multiplied (the 5).
  • The Exponent: How many times to multiply the base by itself (the 3).

How to Read and Calculate

We read 53 as "5 to the power of 3" or "5 cubed." To calculate it, you multiply:

53 = 5 × 5 × 5 = 125
