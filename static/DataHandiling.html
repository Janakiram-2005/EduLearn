Introduction to Data Handling

What is Data?

"Data" is a collection of information, facts, or figures. Think about the scores in a video game, the heights of students in a class, or the number of people who like different ice cream flavors. All of that is data!

The Process of Data Handling

Data handling is the process of working with data to make it useful. It involves a few key steps:

  • Collecting Data: Gathering information.
  • Organizing Data: Arranging information in tables.
  • Representing Data: Showing data visually with graphs.
  • Interpreting Data: Analyzing graphs and tables to draw conclusions.
